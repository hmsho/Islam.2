ISLAM

The word “Islam” means “submission to the will of God.” Islam means to achieve peace – peace with God, peace within oneself, and peace with the creations of God – through wholly submitting oneself to God and accepting His guidance.

Islam Facts

  • Mosques are places where Muslims worship.
  • Muslims worship Allah by praying and reciting the Quran and believe there will be a day of judgment, and life after death.
  • Muslims believe prophets were sent to teach Allah’s law. They respect and believe in some of the same prophets as Jews and Christians, including Abraham, Moses, Noah and Jesus. Muslims believe that Muhammad peace be upon him was the final prophet.

What Are The Five Pillars Of Islam:

The five pillars of Islam are the basic tenets of faith that hold the entire foundation in place. Every follower is obliged to pursue these values and imbibe them. It teaches the way of life and makes it a part of the routine. These practices strengthen the core and unite people in the community.

Shahadah
Shahadah is the term given to making a declaration of faith and dedication to your belief that there is only one God, Allah SWT and that he sent His Messenger, Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) to guide us. As the First Pillar of Islam, Shahadah is one of the most important and acts as your first submission to Islam and announcing your intention as a true follower. Reverts to Islam are also required to make Shahadah as their first act of joining the faith and this serves as an affirmation of their commitment to their faith.To make Shahadah, intent is important, and you must recite the declaration sincerely three times with intent in your heart and mind.
